What you're setting up

Steps

1

Business Portfolio

Go to business.facebook.com.

  • If Orthodox Electric already has a Business Portfolio (a.k.a. Business Manager), use it.
  • If not, create one → name it Orthodox Electric, use Thomas's business email.
2

Create the Texas Solar & Batteries page

In the portfolio: Settings → Pages → Add → Create a new Page.

  • Name: Texas Solar & Batteries
  • Category: Solar Energy Company (or closest)
  • Add a logo, the website txsolarandbatteries.com, and the service area (Bryan–College Station / Brazos Valley).
3

Ad account + payment

In the portfolio: Settings → Ad accounts → Add → Create a new ad account.

  • Name: Texas Solar & Batteries
  • Add Thomas's card as the payment method (the client pays Meta directly for ad spend).
4

Give Vendi Local access Vendi needs this

Enter Vendi Local's Business ID: 2035303137072606
