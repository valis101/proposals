01.Why move off WordPress

The short version of what we discussed on the phone.

The platform wins

  • Faster pages = big SEO boost. Next.js serves pre-rendered HTML, so pages load nearly instantly. Site speed is one of Google's explicit ranking signals — faster site, better rankings.
  • More secure by default. No PHP, no WP admin to brute-force, no theme or plugin exploits. There's just nothing for an attacker to target.
  • Nothing to update or break. No more plugin updates, theme conflicts, or surprise breakages every time WordPress pushes a release.
  • Hosting included — your hosting bill goes away. What you're paying monthly for WordPress hosting today, you stop paying. We host the new site as part of the engagement.
  • Cleaner HTML + structured schema. We add proper schema markup (LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ) so Google can show your business in rich results — another ranking and click-through win.

Adding pages gets way cheaper

  • AI-assisted authoring. Once the page template is built, generating a new service-area page takes minutes — not hours.
  • One template, many pages. Cypress, Tomball, Spring, etc. all share structure with localized copy tuned to each area.
  • $35/page is realistic. That price doesn't exist in WordPress with a designer or developer. The new stack is what makes it work.
  • Easy to keep adding. When you want page #21, #22, #50 — same workflow, same price. Want to expand into Sugar Land later? Done in an afternoon.

What's on every page

  • Localized hero and service copy tuned to that specific city — not a one-line city-name swap.
  • City-specific FAQ section answering the questions homeowners in that area actually ask before they book.
  • Structured schema markup on every page (FAQPage, LocalBusiness, Service) so search engines and AI tools can read your business cleanly.
